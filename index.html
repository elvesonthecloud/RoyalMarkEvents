ABOUT US

Royal Mark is one of the largest event management agencies in Kollam, Kerala. Established in 2005, we've got proven experience in successfully organizing more than 300 events all over Kerala. We swear by our management and client service principles and that's what makes us different- we allow nothing to come in between us working to give our clients the best. Hence, we are dedicated to making your dream event a reality. We bring together clear cut objectives, ultra-careful planning, efficient and satisfying organization and management to give you a smooth, memorable, and delightful event experience. We have dedicated departments for every service. We ensure your cent percent satisfaction as our clients, as your happiness is our priority and we put our heart and soul into it.
